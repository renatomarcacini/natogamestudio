Deep Deep Deep Nightmare

Deep Deep Deep Nightmare is a cute and creepy top-down shooter game. Survive by facing monsters in the nightmare of a boy who didn't heed his mother's warnings about eating too much before bedtime can cause nightmares. Unlock various uniques weapons and choose three weapons for each playthrough as you attempt to defeat the bosses of nightmare!
